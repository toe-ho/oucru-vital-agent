# 01 — Brand Foundation

**Last Updated:** 2026-05-30

## Emotional Core

The product's job is **trust in an automated quality verdict on a medical
signal**. A researcher or reviewer is deciding whether to believe a machine's
accept / reject / uncomputable call on an ECG or PPG recording.

Every brand choice — visual and verbal — must serve that moment of trust. The
brand must feel **precise, calm, and honest about uncertainty**. It must not
feel like flashy AI, nor like sterile hospital software.

## Positioning

| Aspect | Statement |
|--------|-----------|
| **What it is** | An agentic signal-quality assessment tool for ECG/PPG recordings. |
| **Who it serves** | Clinical researchers, reviewers, data engineers, admins. |
| **What it replaces** | Slow, subjective manual visual inspection of waveforms. |
| **Why it's trusted** | Every verdict is backed by named SQI metrics + thresholds, fully auditable, with an explicit "uncomputable" path instead of false confidence. |
| **Relationship to OUCRU** | Standalone product; **developed at OUCRU**, credited as origin — not framed as an internal-only institutional tool. |

## Name & Lockup

Official name is **OUCRU Vital Agent**. Use the right form for the context:

| Context | Form |
|---------|------|
| Formal / legal / first mention | `OUCRU Vital Agent` |
| Product wordmark | `OUCRU` (small tracked caps, above/before) + **`Vital Agent`** |
| Compressed (favicon, mobile header, tight UI, chat self-reference) | **`Vital Agent`** |
| Origin credit (footer/about only) | "Developed at OUCRU — Oxford University Clinical Research Unit." |

**Rules**
- Chatbot refers to itself as **"Vital Agent"** — never "the agent", "the AI",
  or "your assistant".
- Lead with **"Vital"** in compressed contexts; pair tightly with the mark so
  "Agent" reads as product, not generic AI jargon.

### Known name tension (tracked)

"OUCRU" is an institutional acronym and "Agent" is dated AI jargon — both pull
against the standalone-product goal. Current mitigation: lockup + compression +
mark-led identity. **Re-open the name decision only if the product launches
externally** beyond OUCRU.

## Brand Personality

| Trait | Means | Does NOT mean |
|-------|-------|---------------|
| **Precise** | Exact numbers, named metrics, clear thresholds | Cold or robotic in human-facing copy |
| **Calm** | Steady, unhurried, no alarm-language | Slow or vague |
| **Honest** | Surfaces uncertainty; refuses to overclaim | Apologetic or hedging excessively |
| **Engineered** | Instrument-grade, dependable, consistent | Sterile or clinical-by-default everywhere |

## Audience Snapshot (who the brand talks to)

- **Researcher** — batch-processes recordings; wants speed + defensible results.
- **Reviewer** — validates verdicts, overrides segments; wants evidence + audit trail.
- **Admin** — configures thresholds, manages users; wants control + clarity.
- **Readonly** — audits/external; wants to understand at a glance.

The brand defaults to serving the **reviewer's scrutiny** — if it earns the
skeptical reviewer's trust, it earns everyone's.
